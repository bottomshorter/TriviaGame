Lord of the Rings Trivia Game

Upon pressing "START GAME", you will be prompted to answer 5 questions about the Lord of the Rings Trilogy

You will have 10 seconds per questions to click on the correct answer. If you choose the wrong answer, your choice will be in red, and the correct answer will be green. If you don't choose an answer at all the correct answer will show up green.
